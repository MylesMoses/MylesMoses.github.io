Comparing Dinosaurs

Learn about the fascinating differences between avian and non-avian dinosaurs.

Differences

  • Extinction

    Non-avian dinosaurs became extinct after the Cretaceous–Paleogene extinction event, while avian dinosaurs mostly survived and evolved into modern-day birds.

  • Bone Structure

    Avian dinosaurs had hollow bones to aid in flight, whereas non-avian dinosaurs had dense bones to support their weight.

Did You Know?

Some theories suggest that avian dinosaurs survived due to their larger brains and varied diets compared to their non-avian counterparts.
